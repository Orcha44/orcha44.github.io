Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how our applications and services handle user information.

Our games do not collect personally identifiable information directly. Some anonymous data may be collected by third-party services used to improve gameplay experience.

Third-Party Services

The game may use third-party services such as analytics or advertising providers. These services may collect anonymous usage data such as:

Advertising

Advertisements shown in the game may be provided by third-party ad networks which may collect anonymous identifiers for ad personalization and performance measurement.

Children's Privacy

Our applications are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
